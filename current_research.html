Machine Learning the Initial Condtions of the Universe

Tools: statistical/mathematical modeling, machine learning techniques, comological simulations etc Application: physics/cosmology... In the aim of creating a digital twin of the universe and increasing our Pphysical understanding of universe, we can: (1) gather more data (from telescopes; galaxy positions in the sky and CMB (that sets a prior on our initial conditions)) (2) develop tools to deal with the data (that's me) Postulates (and experimentally verified) we rely on: (1) light speed constant, (2) galaxies form in dark matter halos (what is dark matter??)
