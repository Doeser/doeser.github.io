On the hunt for answers to the bigger questions

With a curious mind and a Master's degree in Theoretical Physics from KTH Royal Institute of Technology, I gladly devote myself to studying and unravelling the mysteries and peculiarites of our Universe. Since I successfully defended my master thesis on A Machine Learning Approach for Comprehending Cosmic Expansion I've been working at the Department of Physics at Stockholm University as a Research Engineer. In particular, I've been responsible for developing a statistical package in Python to be used for spectral timing analysis, which is a primal tool for analyzing high-energy radiation from e.g. galactic X-ray binaries and black hole candidates.
